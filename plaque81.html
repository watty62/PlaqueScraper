Provost Ross' House (1594 - Present)

Aberdeen Maritime Museum, Shiprow

Central Aberdeen

The house is said to be the work of Master Mason Andrew Jamesone, father of the painter, George (see separate entry). In the eighteenth century it became the town house of John Ross of Arnage, one of Aberdeens Lord Provosts. After being restored by the National Trust for Scotland in the 1950s it became part of Aberdeens Maritime Museum in the 1990s.
