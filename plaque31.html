James Matthews (1820 - 1898)

15 Albyn Terrace

Central Aberdeen

Architect and Lord Provost of Aberdeen. Educated at Robert Gordons Hospital he began his career as an assistant with Archibald Simpsons Architectural Practice. He set up a partnership with A. Marshall MacKenzie and designed Albyn Terrace, Aberdeen Music Hall, the Tivoli Theatre and Langstane Kirk. Matthews entered local government in 1863 and was elected Lord Provost in 1883.
