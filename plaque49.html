James Fenton Wyness (1903 - 1974)

45 Salisbury Terrace

North Aberdeen

Architect and Historian. He is remembered as an architect and antiquarian who was committed to local issues and one who specialised in the restoration of historic buildings. He was the project architect, editor and historical advisor for the Deeside Field and published a history of Aberdeen, City by the Grey North Sea, in 1965. He became the County Commissioner for the Boy Scouts and was awarded the Silver Wolf, Scoutings highest honour in 1973. He was also a Knight of the Order of St John.
