Drum's Lane

Upperkirkgate

Central Aberdeen

This commemorates the location of Lady Drums Hospital. In 1633 Marion Douglas, Lady Drum, mortified the sum of 3,000 merks for a commodious house for poor widows and aged virgins. Building began in 1671. By 1721 the house also accommodated daughters of Burgesses of Guild. In 1798 the area was redeveloped and Drums Lane was laid out.
