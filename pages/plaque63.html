Sir Alexander Anderson (1802 - 1887)

North Pier

North Aberdeen

Financier and City Planner. Born in Strichen and educated at Aberdeen Grammar School and Marischal College he launched commercial designs for the North of Scotland Insurance Company and the North of Scotland Bank in 1837.
