James Chalmers (1713 - 1764)

Aberdeen Journals Building, Lang Stracht

North Aberdeen

Founder of Aberdeen Journal. Chalmers was apprenticed to printers in Aberdeen and London and then appointed Town printer in Aberdeen in 1736, where he edited and printed the first edition of the Aberdeen Journal in 1748.
